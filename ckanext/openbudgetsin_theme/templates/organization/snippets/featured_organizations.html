• Combined Budget (Union & States)

    This section has a lot of useful documents and datasets on - combined receipts and expenditure by the Union and State Governments; it also offers time series data for several important fiscal indicators for the country.

  • Union Budget

    This section has a lot of useful documents and datasets on - the receipts and expenditure by the Union Government of India - over the last two decades.

  • State Budgets

    This section has the budget documents - covering all detailed budget books - for nearly all States for the last several financial years. It also presents the budget documents for some of the States in a machine-readable format.

  • Municipal Corporation Budgets

    This section has machine-readable datasets on - the receipts and expenditures by a large number of Municipal Corporations in India.

  • District Treasury

    This section presents the actual expenditure flows for the years covered, which happened through the District Treasuries, in select districts across select States. More such Geo-Specific Dashboards will be added in the coming months.
