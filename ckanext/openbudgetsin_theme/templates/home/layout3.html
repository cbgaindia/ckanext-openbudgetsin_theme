Union Budget Explorer

Union Budget Explorer is a dashboard for exploring data, visualizations and insights on Union Budget.
It incorporates data from Budget at a Glance, Expenditure Budget, Receipts Budget and Schemes on Major Central Schemes.

Budget Basics

Tiers of Government

Tiers of government refer to distinct levels of government set up for a particular country. In India, there are three tiers of government: Union, State and Local, the latter being either urban or rural.
